About DealDeli

Our Story

DealDeli was founded by a group of passionate shoppers who were frustrated by the endless hunt for the best grocery prices. Tired of switching between apps, flyers, and store aisles, they imagined a smarter way: a single platform that brings together all the deals, savings, and comparisons you need to shop with confidence.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to affordable groceries. Our mission is to empower shoppers with transparent, real-time price comparisons and smart savings tools. By making prices visible and easy to compare, we help you stretch your budget further—without sacrificing quality or convenience.

What Makes DealDeli Different?

  • Comprehensive Price Comparisons: Instantly compare prices for thousands of grocery items across all your favourite local stores.
  • Personalized Shopping Lists: Build your grocery list and see where you can get each item for less—tailored to your shopping habits.
  • Smart Savings Suggestions: Our intelligent engine recommends cheaper alternatives and highlights limited-time deals.
  • Real-Time Updates: We update our prices continuously, so you always have the latest information.
  • Community-Driven: We listen to your feedback and regularly add new stores and features based on what you want.
  • Transparent & Unbiased: DealDeli is independent—our comparisons are always honest, with no hidden fees or sponsored results.

Our Vision for the Future

We’re just getting started. In the coming months, we’ll be rolling out even more features: price-drop alerts, barcode scanning, expanded store coverage, and a vibrant community space for sharing tips and deals. Your feedback shapes our roadmap—let us know what you want to see next!
